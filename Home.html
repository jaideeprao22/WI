Telangana · preventive public health

The state's eye on public health.

Nethra listens to what people on the ground actually report, forecasts how a rumour or a disease moves across districts, picks the response that works, and sends a vetted Telugu message through the messenger people trust — then learns from what comes back.

Open the console → Explore the modules ↓

Demonstration build. Forecasts are an illustrative model running on real district populations — not a validated prediction. Doctor profiles in Aarogya Darpan are clearly-labelled samples, not real practitioners. Runs offline on a single device.
