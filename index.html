Hear the village. Forecast the harm. Act before it spreads.

Most health systems learn about a rumour or an outbreak after it has already cost someone. This console closes the loop end to end: it structures lived reports from the field, forecasts how a belief or a disease moves across districts, rehearses which response works, and sends a Telugu message through the messenger people trust — then learns from what comes back.

12 — Triage & follow-up

Patient queue — register, triage, never lose a danger sign
