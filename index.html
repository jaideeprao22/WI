01 — Listen & structure

Lived reality — the field knowledge bank

The raw material no frontier model has: what a real household believed, what they did, and what happened. Each report is captured as a Lived-Reality Knowledge Unit (LRKU) — consented, structured, and ready to feed the model. Add one and watch it flow into the signals and the map.

Aligned with IDSP / IHIP. These field reports map to Form S (syndromic) — symptom signals from ASHAs & ANMs, captured before lab confirmation. Form P (presumptive, by medical officers) and Form L (laboratory-confirmed) feed in from facilities. Designed to align with national reporting — not a live IHIP feed in this demo.

13 — Verified access

Aarogya Darpan Telangana — verified doctor map & public-health access grade

A citizen-facing doctor access network: verify before visit, find registered/claimed doctor profiles on a map, calculate a safer Public Health Access Grade, collect structured patient experience signals, and highlight districts where verified doctor information is missing. This is not a clinical-quality ranking and not a defamation board.

Illustrative DEMO data. Every profile here is a fictional “Dr. Demo …” sample with a placeholder TSMC-DEMO number — not a real practitioner. Real verification must use the official NMC National Medical Register and the Telangana State Medical Council. This is a transparency & access concept, not a clinical-quality or “best/worst doctor” ranking.
